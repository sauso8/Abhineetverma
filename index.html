About Me

Dr. Abhineet Verma

"Dr. Abhineet Verma is a highly successful academician with a strong history in undergraduate and graduate studies, as well as a Ph.D. from Banaras Hindu University (BHU) Varanasi. His doctorate thesis, "Towards Modulation of NIR Emission: Design, Synthesis, Structural, and Photophysical Studies of Lanthanide Complexes," demonstrates his proficiency in the complex fields of lanthanide chemistry and near-infrared emission and emission. Dr. Verma is currently involved in research and academia as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Chemistry at MNIT Jaipur. He is also the editorial board member of ‘American Journal of Applied Science’. His work exemplifies a dedication to excellence in education and scientific endeavours, as he exhibits a steadfast commitment to expanding our understanding of near-infrared emission."
